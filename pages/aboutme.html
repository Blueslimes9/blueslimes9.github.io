about me

hello! my name is ben, i was born in late 2008 and am currently 16.

i have no pets, two older brothers (one lives in florida), and a younger sister

i grew up and live in the great state of maine, USA, which has its ups and downs.

maine is one of the safest states, but the tourism here is heavy, and the infrastructure isnt really equipped to support it, traffic in the summer here is a nightmare.

maine is generally pretty cold, which i prefer over being hot, and the winters here are very white.


i went to a small ish elementary school, there were only around 18 people in my class.

my elementary and jr high was combined into one school.

when covid hit, (~6th grade) my mother changed jobs to become a teacher in a different school

me and my sister had to tag along with her, so we switched schools.

this school was even smaller then the first one, and i don’t really talk to the people from there anymore.

everyone was very nice and it wasnt really that hard to fit in.

eventually the pandemic ended and i was able to return to my old school.

my town is lucky enough to have no high school, so i got to choose where i wanted to go in the nearby area, and the town pays for tuition.

ofc i chose a collage prep private school :P hey, if you’re gonna pay for my tuition, you bet i'm going to some expensive one.


and thats where i am today, i haven’t been alive for all that long in the grand scheme of things, and compared to others it is relatively boring.


im also a cat person, dogs are ok, and i get that they are “mans best friend,” but i don’t think any dog can recreate that feeling when a cat crawls on to your lap and takes a eep, or atleast, i've never had that happen.
